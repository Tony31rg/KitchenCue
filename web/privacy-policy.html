Privacy Policy

App: KitchenCue
Last Updated: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KitchenCue ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile and web application.

By using KitchenCue, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information to provide the service:

  • Staff account details (for example, staff ID, display name, and role such as waiter or kitchen).
  • Operational data (orders, menu items, stock levels, table assignments, and status updates).
  • Technical and log data (basic device and app usage information for reliability and security).
  • Media files you upload (for example, menu item images).

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Authenticate users and manage access based on staff roles.
  • Provide real-time restaurant operations (orders, kitchen queue, stock updates).
  • Store and sync data across authorized devices.
  • Improve app stability, performance, and security.

3. Data Storage and Services

KitchenCue uses Firebase services (such as Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Storage) to store and process data.

Your data is handled according to this Privacy Policy and the applicable Firebase/Google terms.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data only:

  • With service providers required to operate the app infrastructure (for example Firebase).

  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the service.

5. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for operational and legal purposes. Data may be deleted or anonymized when no longer required.

6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

KitchenCue is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. Contact us to submit a request.
